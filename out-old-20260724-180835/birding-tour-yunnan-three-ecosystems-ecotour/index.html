Yunnan Endemic Birding — Three Ecosystems

Seven-day Birding Tour

Yunnan Endemic
Birding

Three ecosystems, one critically-endemic bird

A focused 7-day loop through northwest Yunnan's three most distinct birding ecosystems — Lashi highland wetland, Laojun Mountain mid-elevation oak-rhododendron forest, and Baihualing sub-tropical valleys. The headline target is the Biet's Laughingthrush (Garrulax bieti), found only here and reliably seen at the Laojun Mountain birding station we partner with. Small group (2-4 guests), expert local guides, 200-300 species target.

What Makes This Tour Special

Tour Highlights

Garrulax bieti

Biet's Laughingthrush Anchor Day

Two full mornings at the Laojun Mountain birding station with a local guide who can reliably bring the critically-endemic Biet's Laughingthrush into view. The species is restricted to NW Yunnan and has not been recorded in significant numbers anywhere else.

No flights, no wasted time

Three-Ecosystem Loop

A clean three-stop linear arc through three distinct ecosystems — highland wetland, mid-elevation montane forest, and sub-tropical valley — with no internal flights. Total driving ~14-15 hours across the week, all between 25 min and 5-6 hours.

Conservation-first guide practice

IUCN-Aligned Operation

Cloud Mountain's lead coordinator holds the 2024 IUCN-CEC Regional Award for Asia. Trip design follows IUCN sustainable-tourism guidelines: pre-tripped optics, locally-owned homestays, payments to the Laojun and Baihualing birding stations, and no factory-shop commissions.
DETAILED SCHEDULE

Tour Itinerary

  • Pickup at Lijiang Sanyi Airport (LJG) or your Old Town hotel (10 AM-noon arrivals), 30-min transfer to a boutique Naxi courtyard in Lijiang Old Town - check-in, light brunch, settle in. After lunch, 25-min drive to Lashi Wetland Park for the conservation context session at the Lashi Bird Museum and Conservation Bureau, hosted by the local conservation biologist who manages the wetland's crane wintering program. Pre-dusk birding on the Lashi causeways - the second-best window of the day - Common Crane and Black Stork flight back to their roost at sunset, golden-hour light. Welcome dinner at a Naxi courtyard restaurant in Sanyuan village, trip briefing and checklist primer with the lead guide, and an optional 30-45 min owl listening session along the wetland edge (Collared Scops Owl near-guaranteed, Mountain Scops Owl possible).
  • 5:30 AM out with the local guide - dawn at Lashi is the anchor wetland session, cranes leave roost at first light, waterfowl flights, passerines in the reedbeds. This is the productive wetland window of the entire trip. 9:00 AM brunch and checklist review, then 9:30 AM drive Lijiang to Laojun Mountain (1.5 hr, elevation 2,400m to ~2,800m). 11:00 AM lunch at Li Ming village, a Lisu-minority village in the Laojun foothills, followed by an approach walk to the birding station - ~30 min into mid-elevation oak-rhododendron forest. First light Biet's Laughingthrush (Garrulax bieti) possible if quiet. Village homestay dinner and briefing on the Biet's Laughingthrush plan for tomorrow.
  • 5:30 AM coffee + biscuits, out with the local Laojun station guide. 6:00-11:00 AM full morning at the station - Biet's Laughingthrush is the headline, the local guide knows the call playback and positioning that brings it in. 11:00 AM-3:00 PM lunch break at the village homestay, nap, lead guide walks guests through the morning's eBird checklist. 3:00-6:00 PM afternoon session on a lower-elevation trail for different species - Golden Bush Robin, White-browed Shortwing, and mixed flocks of fulvettas and sibias. Dinner with the Laojun Lisu village elder - he tells the story of how the birding station started.
  • 5:30-10:00 AM final morning at the Laojun station - a different micro-habitat walk today, chase species missed on Day 3. 10:30 AM drive Laojun to Baihualing via the Hangrui Expressway (5-6 hours), lunch stop at a highway town. Afternoon drive drops through the Salween (Nujiang) river gorge - optional stop at the Nu River overlook for raptors. Late afternoon arrival at Baihualing village, check into a village homestay, meet Hou Tiguo's team. Briefing on Baihualing's 520+ species and dinner at the homestay with a local Lisu-style meal.
  • 5:30 AM out with Hou Tiguo's team for the birding station - the famous Baihualing hide setup. Baihualing is lower elevation, hotter, more humid - different species mix from Laojun. Targets: Red-tailed Laughingthrush, Rusty-capped Fulvetta, Silver-eared Mesia, Red-billed Leiothrix, plus mixed-flocks of warblers and flycatchers. 3:00-5:00 PM different trail - the Laifengshan approach for Silver Pheasant and mixed-flocks of laughingthrushes, bird checklist and eBird upload with the lead guide. Village homestay dinner.
  • 5:30-10:00 AM final Baihualing morning - local guides know which micro-site is best for the species we missed on Day 5. 10:30 AM drive back to Lijiang (5-6 hours), lunch stop at Yongping - clean highway-town restaurant. Late afternoon arrival in Lijiang Old Town, check into a boutique hotel. Farewell dinner at a Naxi courtyard restaurant, trip list review - the lead guide prints a one-page eBird trip-list for each guest.
  • Optional Naxi Old Town walk for guests who want one last cultural experience, coffee at a Lijiang Old Town cafe. Then transfer to Lijiang Sanyi Airport (LJG) for departure flights - or, optional half-day add-on: drive to Lashi for one final wetland morning if guests have a late flight. Trip end.
