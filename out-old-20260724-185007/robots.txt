# ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended, etc.). The explicit per-bot sections
# below are defensive — they make the policy unambiguous for AI crawlers and
# prevent accidental future over-blocking in the Cloudflare AI Crawl Control
# dashboard from silently re-disallowing them.
#
# For AI-specific discovery (Markdown for Agents, llms.txt) we also publish
# /llms.txt and /llms-full.txt — see https://llmstxt.org for the spec.

# Content Signals (https://contentsignals.org/) — explicit AI usage preferences.
# - ai-train=no: do not use our content to train AI models
# - search=yes: allow AI search engines to use our content for retrieval
# - ai-input=yes: allow AI assistants to ingest our content for RAG
# - use=reference: when surfaced, treat as a reference (cite, don't paraphrase-only)
# This is the most permissive stance for being cited by AI assistants while
# still protecting against unauthorized training.
Content-Signal: ai-train=no, search=yes, ai-input=yes, use=reference
