For Schools & Education

Project-Based Learning programs that create real-world impact and lasting memories.

Cloud Mountain's educational programs are designed for international schools and educational institutions looking to provide transformative learning experiences.

Our programs align with IB, AP, and A-Level curricula, featuring Project-Based Learning (PBL) methodology that connects classroom knowledge to real-world conservation and sustainability challenges.

Program Types

Middle & High School

Student EcoCamps

Multi-day immersive camps for student groups. Focus on leadership development, teamwork, and environmental conservation.

For Families with Kids 5+

Family EcoCamps

Quality bonding time for families while teaching children about nature, sustainability, and cultural heritage.

Curriculum Alignment

Our programs support multiple educational frameworks

IB Programme

CAS (Creativity, Activity, Service) hours, Extended Essay research, and Theory of Knowledge connections.

AP & A-Level

Environmental Science, Geography, and Biology field study components with documented learning outcomes.

SDGs Integration

United Nations Sustainable Development Goals alignment. Students contribute to real conservation projects.

Service Learning

Meaningful community service integrated with reflection and academic content for holistic education.
